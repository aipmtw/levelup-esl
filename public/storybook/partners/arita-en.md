# Partner — Arita (teacher / 1-to-1-with-parents channel) — **master partner**

> Arita is one of Mark's **two direct master partners**. She can recruit and coach sub-partners (e.g. fellow English teachers with their own student bases) under her umbrella via the master-partner model. See [`../concept/master-partner-model-en.md`](../concept/master-partner-model-en.md). Mark bills Arita; Arita bills her sub-partners however she wants.

## Who she is
Arita is an **English teacher** who **already has her own kid students in the 0–12 range**. She is both the content designer AND the trusted adult in each student's life.

## Target
Her **existing kid students**, age 0–12, plus whoever her students' parents refer to her.

## Go-to-market channel
- **Teacher-to-parent 1:1 consultation.** For each specific student, Arita discusses with the kid's parent:
  - current English level
  - interests
  - learning goal (vocabulary area, grammar focus, comprehension, confidence-building, …)
  - tone / difficulty preference
- Then she generates a **personalized teaching-aid book** for that student, approved by the parent.
- Result: each book is a **custom teaching aid**, not a generic storybook. Higher perceived value per book.

## Languages
- **Traditional Chinese (zh-TW / zh-Hant)** — baseline, for bilingual explanations and parent readability. Traditional only, never Simplified.
- **English (en)** — primary instructional language.
- **Japanese (ja-JP)** — optional.

## What Arita owns
- Her existing teacher-student-parent relationships.
- Her teaching methodology and book style.
- Her per-book pricing (likely higher than Audrey's, given 1:1 custom nature).
- Her **public** partner page `app.markluce.ai/arita` — target market + ad copy.
- Her **private workspace** — student list, per-student parent-consultation notes, allowlists, book inventory. Not visible to other partners.

## What Mark provides
- Same shared kid book engine as Audrey.
- Templates tuned for teaching-aid use (vocabulary highlight, comprehension questions, exercise pages, parent notes section).
- A **parent consultation questionnaire** template.
- PWA gating by LINE account — only the student's family device(s) play the book.
