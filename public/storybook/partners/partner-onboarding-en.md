# Partner onboarding

How a new partner joins markluce.ai and what Mark provides.

## Steps
1. **Intro call** — Mark + partner align on niche, language mix, end-user audience, and pricing model (partner's choice).
2. **Slug reservation** — partner picks `{slug}` for `app.markluce.ai/{slug}`.
3. **Terms** — partner signs `pricing/partner-terms.md` (B2B fee structure).
4. **Page setup** — Mark provisions the partner page, partner fills in copy/brand/CTA via the CMS.
5. **Templates fork** — partner picks base questionnaire + book layout + art style + voice pack; customizes from there.
6. **Demo works** — partner generates 2–3 demo books (or lessons) to showcase on their page.
7. **LINE group** — partner sets up their own LINE group (Mark provides the setup guide + OA bot template).
8. **Launch** — partner shares `app.markluce.ai/{slug}` with their audience; attribution flows via LINE Login `state` param.

## What Mark provides at onboarding
- Platform access (shared infra).
- Base templates (questionnaires, layouts, voices, art styles).
- Partner page + CMS.
- LINE OA bot template + group setup guide.
- Analytics dashboard (usage, generation counts, attribution).
- Direct support channel with Mark during onboarding.

## What the partner brings
- Their own audience / community.
- Their own niche and content direction.
- Their own end-user pricing + monetization.
- Their own brand voice.
