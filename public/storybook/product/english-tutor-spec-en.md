# English tutor spec (Arita's legacy surface — secondary)

> **Status:** secondary / experimental. Arita's M1 focus is the **kid-teacher product** (see [`kid-book-spec-en.md`](kid-book-spec-en.md) and [`../partners/arita-en.md`](../partners/arita-en.md)). This doc describes her **legacy adult-learner surface** at `markluce.ai/arita`, kept live but not M1 priority.

## Product shape (legacy)
Interactive AI English tutor with switchable **domain focus**.

## Domain focus switch
- **TOEIC** — Parts 5 / 6 / 7 practice.
- **Business English** — emails, meetings, negotiation, presentations.
- **Professional / domain-specific** — IT+AI, finance, medical, legal, etc.
- **General** — catch-all conversation practice.

## Features (inherited from current arita page)
- LINE login required.
- Chatbot for Q&A, translation, role-play.
- Azure TTS (Ava / Andrew + others).
- Random prompt suggestions tuned per domain.
- Traditional-Chinese ↔ en bilingual explanations (Traditional only — no Simplified).

## Why kept (not killed)
Arita's adult surface already has traffic. Killing it has no upside. Stays online as a stable v1 while her M1 kid-teacher product ships.
